# Invite a member / switch organizations

**Goal:** work across organizations and manage who belongs to them.

## Switch the active organization

1. Sign in. Your current organization is shown in the **organization switcher** in the
   header.
2. Open the switcher and pick another organization you belong to.
3. The active organization changes; uploads, jobs, reports, and API keys all apply to
   whichever organization is active.

## Invite and manage members (admins)

1. With the target organization active, go to **Members** (`/members`). This is available
   to organization **admins**.
2. Add a member to the organization.
3. Manage existing members — adjust their role (admin or member) or remove them.

!!! note
    A new account created at registration gets its own organization. Members-only actions
    such as inviting people or removing members require the **admin** role in the active
    organization.

## Result

You can move between organizations from the header and, as an admin, control membership
and roles per organization. See also the [User Guide](../user-guide/index.md).
